About CV.json v1.0.0

Proof of Concept application for the MEANT stack, with Mongoose and Passport. The goal is to be able to publish an up to date CV to interested parties. It is a multi-user SPA with simple management options and a public view to share with the public. The code is freely available on Github, but it is not recommended for scaffolding or basing another application on this.
